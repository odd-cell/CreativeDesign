Curriculum roadmap

Work through the phases in order. Mark each certification complete as you finish it and watch your progress bar grow.

  1. Phase 1 · Job-ready foundations

    Become employable fast while building a base for UX and systems work.

    • Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate

      Coursera

      Build a strong base in data analysis to unlock entry-level roles and support UX and product decisions.

      Focus: data, dashboards, data storytelling
      Open course

    • Google UX Design Professional Certificate

      Coursera

      Learn research, interaction design, and Figma workflows that bridge analytics with user-centered design.

      Focus: research, wireframes, prototypes, usability
      Open course

    • Google Cloud Development Certificate

      Coursera

      Understand cloud-native foundations so your projects can scale and integrate with modern platforms.

      Focus: cloud services, deployment, automation · Cloud Architect learning path
      Open course Architect path

  2. Phase 2 · Storytelling & game design toolkit

    Expand your toolkit into narrative, visual storytelling, and core game design.

    • The Art of Visual Storytelling

      CU Boulder · Coursera

      Learn to compose visuals that communicate emotion, clarity, and intent.

      Focus: composition, visual language, pacing
      Open course

    • Story & Narrative Development for Video Games

      CalArts · Coursera

      Build narrative structures, characters, and arcs tailored to interactive media.

      Focus: narrative design, characters, branching
      Open course

    • Game Design: Art & Concepts

      CalArts · Coursera

      Understand mechanics, dynamics, aesthetics, and how game systems create player experiences.

      Focus: systems thinking, mechanics, playtesting
      Open course

  3. Phase 3 · Technical craft & execution

    Focus on concrete skills for shipping games and interactive experiences.

    • Character Design for Video Games

      CalArts · Coursera

      Design appealing, functional characters that support your game’s mechanics and story.

      Focus: silhouettes, readability, style systems
      Open course

    • World Design for Video Games

      CalArts · Coursera

      Create coherent worlds that align narrative, gameplay, and visual tone.

      Focus: level flow, environments, lore
      Open course

    • Build a Pixel Platformer in Godot

      Packt

      Ship a complete 2D platformer while learning Godot’s workflow and scripting.

      Focus: 2D gameplay, pixel art, implementation
      Open course

    • Intro to 3D Game Development in Godot

      Packt

      Learn the fundamentals of 3D scenes, lighting, and gameplay in Godot.

      Focus: 3D workflows, cameras, interaction
      Open course
